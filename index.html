About Us

I am Bivek Raj Shakya, founder of Trexplore. Nepal is a country that relies heavily on tourism, and I have seen firsthand how tourists can be mistreated or scammed by travel agencies. Trexplore was created to provide a safe and reliable way for tourists to travel Nepal.
